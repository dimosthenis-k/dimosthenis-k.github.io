Short History

As far back as 1960, SkyHigh Airways was founded by two people. John Sky and Nathan High were both students
in the School of Aviation of the University of Central Missouri. Both fascinated by anything that had to do with air-travel,
they decided to join forces after graduating and create their own airline, so they could spread their joy and knowledge
about all things flight, with the whole world.


It's been a long trip...

Today, after more than 40 years of flying people around the world, over 200 available destinations and over
150 million passengers served, SkyHigh Airlines continues to provide excellent air-travelling services while
focusing on being a customer-first company.
